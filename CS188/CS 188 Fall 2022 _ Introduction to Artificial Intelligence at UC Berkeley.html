    RRR Week Announcements

    Dec 5
    • Final exam logistics are posted at https://inst.eecs.berkeley.edu/~cs188/fa22/exam.
      • If you need an alternate time, remote exam, or accommodations (e.g. DSP), please fill out the logistics form by Sunday, December 11, 11:59 PM PT.
      • We will try and make a more detailed post about scope once the exam is finalized later this week. We’ll only be lightly testing content that was covered after the strike started.
    • For 1 extra credit point on the final, fill out the course evaluations, and upload proof to Gradescope. Evaluations close on Sunday, December 11, 11:59 PM PT.
    • Instructor office hours this week: Monday through Thursday, 5-6:30pm PT, online.
    • We’ll work out a solution to the missing weeks of discussion attendance after the final exam is over, so please hold any questions about discussion attendance until then.
